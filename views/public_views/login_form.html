Features
Minimalistic Design

Materializecss is the styling framework powering the website. All the ui interacions are designed with minimal room consumption but with maximum functionality. Once logged in take advantage of the smart search that allow you to filter out any event


Electronic Tickets

NFCVT platorm emphaisises on virtuaisation. All tickes obtained on NFCVT are stored in virtual wallet and later utalised through mobile app.


Security

When tickts are traditionaly validated by hand mistakes occour making its unsecure and mistake are likely to occour. All NFCVT validation is performed by the system, this enables to implement high security standars.


Dynamic Managment

NFCVT also gives you an ability to host your own events. This means that you can allow other users to obtain electronic tickets for your event. As an organiser you will have acces to your admin panel from where you will be able fully control your event. Event information will be dynamicaly updated as users check in to the event.


No Hardware

Huge advantage of NFCVT is that no additional hardware is required. As a user you use your mobile device to utalise tickets and as an organiser you also use your mobile device to scann tickets. No more traditional barcode scanners required or other devices
